About the Mechanical Engineering Department

The Mechanical Engineering Department offers quality diploma education in the fields of manufacturing, thermal engineering, machine design, and industrial technology. The department builds strong technical foundations in mechanics, production processes, and maintenance of machines. With experienced faculty and well-equipped workshops and laboratories, students gain hands-on training in machining, welding, fitting, and CAD basics. The curriculum focuses on practical skills, problem-solving, and industry-oriented learning. Our aim is to develop skilled, innovative, and industry-ready diploma engineers capable of contributing to modern manufacturing and engineering sectors.
