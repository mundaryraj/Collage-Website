About the Civil Engineering Department

The Civil Engineering Department provides quality diploma education focused on planning, designing, and constructing infrastructure such as buildings, roads, bridges, and water systems. The department builds strong foundations in structural engineering, construction technology, surveying, and environmental engineering. With experienced faculty and well-equipped laboratories, students gain practical knowledge through fieldwork, site visits, and project-based learning. The curriculum emphasizes technical skills, safety practices, and sustainable development. Our goal is to prepare skilled and responsible diploma engineers capable of contributing to modern infrastructure development and pursuing higher technical education..
